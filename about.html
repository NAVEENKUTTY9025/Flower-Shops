Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bloom & Petal began as a small passion project in a tiny studio. Today, we're a full-service floristry studio creating artful arrangements for all of life's special moments.

Our philosophy is simple: flowers should speak from the heart. We source the freshest seasonal blooms, prioritize sustainable practices, and pour creativity into every stem. Whether it's an intimate bouquet or a grand wedding, we treat each arrangement as a unique work of art.
